FUTURE SIGHT

Worldide Liberation Movement

Social movements after a certain points dies. Either internally, by factionalism, or by heavy repression, or most of the times both, leading to encapsulation, instrumentalisation and assimilation by the same structures they were fighting. What happens when social movement overcome that issue? And how such an event would be saved, archived and transmitted? That is what i explore in Future Sights, where I speculate on such matters and create a timeline recalling the key events of this worldwide change in social and power relations, from a student revolt, to self-managed areas, to new or older ways of handling territories, resources and social interactions. This also happens through the use of propaganda. Then I create a 3D space where the archiving and exhibition of this event can be visited and experienced digitally, with a soundscape fitting each part of the space.
